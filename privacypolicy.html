WildAtlas Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how WildAtlas collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, subscribe to our newsletter, contact us, or interact with our services.

1. Introduction

WildAtlas is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as your name and email address when you contact us or subscribe.
  • Basic usage data such as pages visited, device type, browser type, and time spent on the website.
  • Information you provide through forms, comments, enquiries, or advertising requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Respond to enquiries and support requests.
  • Send newsletters, updates, and site-related information.
  • Improve website performance, design, and content.
  • Manage partnerships, advertising requests, and collaborations.
  • Maintain website security and prevent misuse.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

WildAtlas may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, remember preferences, and better understand how visitors interact with the site.

You can usually control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, deliver newsletters, or manage analytics, only where necessary.

We may also disclose information if required by law or when necessary to protect our website, users, or legal rights.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, advertisers, affiliates, or partner platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse. However, no internet-based system is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data where appropriate.
  • Withdraw consent from marketing communications at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

WildAtlas does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our site, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, technology, or legal obligations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
