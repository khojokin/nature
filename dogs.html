Dog Habits, Reproduction, and Daily Life

Dogs are highly social animals that often live closely with humans. Their daily habits may include sleeping for long hours, exploring through smell, playing, eating on routine, and responding strongly to tone and body language.

A female dog’s pregnancy usually lasts a little over two months, and litter sizes can vary depending on breed, size, age, and health. Some breeds may have small litters, while others can produce much larger ones.

Because dogs adapt so well to human homes, farms, cities, and working environments, they have become one of the most successful and diverse domestic animals in the world.
