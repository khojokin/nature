The Snow Leopard: Ghost of the Mountains

High in the rugged mountains of Central Asia lives one of the most mysterious predators on Earth—the snow leopard.

Snow leopards are skilled hunters capable of leaping up to six times their body length.

Despite the challenges they face, the snow leopard remains one of nature's most fascinating predators.
