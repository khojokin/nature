Planet Facts

Space is full of incredible planetary features, from intense storms to frozen rings and massive magnetic fields.

Rocky Worlds

Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars are rocky planets with solid surfaces and diverse geological features.

Gas Giants

Jupiter and Saturn are made mostly of gas and are much larger than the rocky inner planets.

Ice Giants

Uranus and Neptune are colder outer planets with icy compositions, strong winds, and deep blue colors.
